    About Jaypee University of Engineering & Technology

    Jaypee University of Engineering & Technology (JUET), Guna has been established vide Government of Madhya Pradesh gazette extra ordinary No.3 of 2010 dated 29th April 2010 as a private university in the State of MP under the provisions of MP Niji Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam 2007. The university has been notified by the UGC under section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956 and Accredited by NAAC with Grade “A+” in 2nd Cycle in 2023 and Grade "A" in 1st cycle of Accreditation in 2016. This University is sponsored by Jaypee Group. It was earlier Jaypee Institute of Engineering and Technology, Guna, established in the year 2003 based on the MOU signed between Jaiprakash Sewa Sansthan (a not-for-profit trust) and the Government of Madhya Pradesh with an aim of becoming a Center of Excellence in Engineering and Technology in MP.

    About Center of Excellence for Renewable Energy

    Welcome to the Center of Renewable Energy at Jaypee University of Engineering & Technology!

    Our center is a dynamic hub for pioneering research, education, and innovation in the field of renewable energy. Committed to sustainability and environmental stewardship, we strive to make a meaningful impact on the global energy landscape.

    Vision:

    To achieve excellence as a hub for exploring, educating, and advocating renewable energy, with the goal of providing the university community with the necessary knowledge to excel in research, education, and community engagement within the transformative realm of renewable energy and tools to play a leading role in shaping sustainable solutions.

    Mission:

    1. Pioneering global research, confronting energy challenges for sustainable solutions

    2. Cultivate deep understanding and equip students for renewable energy.

    3. Advocating policies, forging alliances, and engaging communities in sustainable energy missions.

    Key Focus Areas:

    • Research Excellence: We lead impactful research initiatives, exploring innovative technologies and methodologies to address the world's growing energy challenges.

    • Education and Training: Empowering the next generation of leaders, our educational programs equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to drive change in the renewable energy sector.

    • Community Outreach: We believe in making a difference beyond our campus. Through collaborations and projects, we actively contribute to creating a positive environmental impact in local communities.

    Facilities:

    Our state-of-the-art facilities provide an ideal environment for hands-on learning and experimentation, fostering a culture of innovation and sustainability.

    Collaborations:

    Collaboration is key to our success. We actively seek partnerships with industry leaders, research institutions, and governmental bodies to drive impactful advancements in renewable energy.

    About Our Policies

    • Presentation Requirement

      To ensure proper registration and publication, following requirements must be met:

      1. Author Registration: At least one author must be registered by paying the registration fee.

      2. Paper and Presentation Limit: One author registration can cover maximum two (2) technical presentations or submissions along with two (2 additional poster presentations).

      3. Presentations: If author intends to present more papers for example two (2) papers or presentation, they request a co-author registration.

      4. Poster Presentations: Only students can present the poster presentations, the author (student) can register at appropriate student registration fee before the registration deadline.

      5. Student Registration Requirements: Student registrations are exclusively available to full-time undergraduate, graduate students and post graduate students. Proof of student status, such as a copy of the student identification card or a letter from the head of the institute/ registrar, is required to register as student.

      6. Presenter Attendance Policy: In order for a paper to be published in the official archival proceedings, it must be presented at the conference by an author or co-author. These proceedings are registered with the Library of conference, abstracted and indexed.

      7. Conference Format: Technical conference will be conducted as in-person events.

      8. In-Person Requirement: Video and/or zoom presentations will not be accepted as substitutes for in-person presentations during conferences.

    • Code of conduct/Anti-Harassment

      1. Centre of Excellence for Renewable Energy of JUET, Guna is committed to providing a safe, productive, and inclusive environment that is free from any form of unlawful discrimination or harassment. This includes but is not limited to, behavior based on race, national origin, citizenship, sex, gender identity or expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, or familial status. Harassment can take different forms such as verbal comments, jokes, imagery, unwanted physical contact, or sexual attention.

      2. If you have any concerns related to this policy, or if you witness or experience any conduct that you believe violates this policy, please immediately contact anCOE-RE staff member or send an email to coere@juetguna.in. You can also report concerns anonymously to the COE-RE at 1800121884444

      3. COE-RE reserves the right to determine, at its sole discretion, if a behavior violates this policy. Consequences for violating this policy may include a warning, removal from or denial of access to COE-RE-sponsored meetings and events, temporary or permanent exclusion from future COE-RE-sponsored meetings or events, or other appropriate actions. Please note that no refunds will be issued to any participant who is asked to leave a meeting due to a violation of this policy.

    • Cancellation and Substitutions

      1. Cancellations and substitutions are allowed. However, full registration fees will be charged unless a cancellation notice is sent in writing, and received or faxed on or before 1 November 2024 to the registration chair. All the registrations for published papers in the Proceedings cannot be cancelled.

      2. A 200 USD handling fee will be applied to all cancelled registrations. Fees cannot be refunded for registrations cancelled after the deadline. No-shows will be billed.
